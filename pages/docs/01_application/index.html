User flows

Visitor:

Citizen:

  • Apply for elections
  • Vote on candidates
  • Vote on Voting
  • Read pro/con articles and complete quiz
  • Read article responses and complete quiz

Political actor:

  • Start new voting
  • Publish and assign article to voting
  • Publish and assign response to article
  • Get winner voting budget money

Administrator:

  • Manage citizenship applications
  • Schedule next elections
  • Close finished elections
  • Declare first voting cycle start date
  • Assign content check quiz to voting
  • Assign content check quiz to article
  • Assign content check quiz to response
  • Approve voting
  • Grant/revoke admin role
  • Revoke citizen role
